Portal Master

Portal Master is a teleporter NPC, which has 130+ teleports.
It includes all zones in wow.

The teleporter has a nice design and it has been in use for years on many servers.

Portal Master was created with blizzlike feeling and usability in mind.

Unlike teleporters you normally find, it doesnt use colors or weird icons in menus, which usually looks bad.

Made for 3.3.5a.
