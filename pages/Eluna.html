Eluna is a Lua engine for TrinityCore and MaNGOS

The engine uses Lua 5.2 and is developed by a small group

Lua allows the user to make simple and advanced scripts with Lua

You do not need to re compile your server to use the script. A simple restart is enough.

Please do note that the engine is being worked on.
